Caprese Salad

Fresh and Tasty Salad

This caprese salad with tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil is drizzled with a sweet balsamic reduction and olive oil. Easy, impressive, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Balsamic vinegar and honey
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  • Gather all ingredients.
  • Make a balsamic reduction with the balsamic vinegar and honey.
  • Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving plate with basil.
  • Drizzle with balsamic reduction and honey.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy!
